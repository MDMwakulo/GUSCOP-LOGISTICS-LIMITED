Providing Quality Logistics

At Guscop, we ensure that our products are delivered to our clients in the freshest and most efficient manner possible. We work with reliable logistics partners, such as PML, who specialize in handling perishable goods to ensure that our products are shipped quickly and efficiently to your destination, wherever you are in the world.

Our products are sorted, and packed to preserve their freshness and quality. They are transported to the Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA) where they are loaded onto temperature-controlled storage spaces for transportation. Our logistics partners ensure that our products are transported in the most efficient and sustainable way possible, using eco-friendly packaging materials and optimizing routes to minimize transport time.

When you choose us as your farm export partner, you can trust that we will take care of every aspect of the transportation process, ensuring that your products arrive on time and in the freshest possible condition. Thank you for choosing us, and we look forward to serving you!
